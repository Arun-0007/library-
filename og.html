About Bookoholic

Bookoholic started with a simple dream — to create a reading space where knowledge meets passion. It began as a small collection of books for curious minds and grew into a community of dedicated readers. Our goal is to make books accessible, affordable, and enjoyable for everyone. From science to fiction, from romance to crime, we carefully curate every category. We believe reading builds imagination, improves focus, and shapes future leaders. Over time, Bookoholic has expanded its collection and introduced smart subscription plans. Our QR-based system allows readers to explore books instantly. We continuously update trending and most-loved titles. Bookoholic is not just a library — it’s a reader’s second home. Join us and be part of a growing reading revolution.
